<Birb Games>


The new Swapdeck has just released but your parents will not get it for you. You have only one choice now: mow lawns and save the cash you earn to buy it yourself. The weeds are more aggressive than ever and it's going to be dangerous work. Will you be able to get the console of your dreams or will you succumb to the dangers of Neighbor Bob's lawn?

Features:

  • Many different types of weeds that all want to kill you.
  • Shrub demons, killer rabbits, and wasps that also all want to kill you.
  • Flowers! (that want to kill you)
  • 20 different neighbors that will pay you to mow their lawn.
  • A water gun to fight back against all the things that want to kill you.
  • A store to buy items to make killing your enemies easier (and I guess to help you mow lawns more efficiently or something).
  • Ice cream!
